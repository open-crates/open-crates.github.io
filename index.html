Playlists in. Serato crates out.

Open Crates is a desktop app that converts playlists from Spotify, YouTube, and Apple Music into Serato crates — so your library stays DJ-ready.

Get the app (soon) Read the docs

How it works

1

Connect your music service (Spotify / YouTube / Apple Music).

2

Select a playlist and choose export options.

3

Export a Serato crate and start mixing.

Note: API credentials are handled securely. Never paste secrets into public repos.

Built for DJs

Designed around crates, not spreadsheets. Keep your prep workflow fast and repeatable.

Local-first

The app runs on your machine. You control the library and the output files.
