Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-28

Open Crates is a desktop app that helps users convert playlists from supported music services into Serato crates. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

What we collect

  • Authentication tokens (e.g., OAuth access/refresh tokens) required to access your connected music services.
  • Playlist metadata you choose to import (playlist name, track list, and related IDs/URLs).
  • App diagnostics (optional): basic error logs to help troubleshoot crashes or export failures.

Where your data is processed

Open Crates is designed to run locally on your machine. When you connect third-party services, your browser may be redirected to those services for login and consent.

Third-party services

Open Crates integrates with third-party APIs (e.g., Spotify, YouTube, Apple Music). Your use of those services is also governed by their respective privacy policies and terms.

Data retention

Tokens and imported playlist data are stored locally to provide the service. You can disconnect accounts and remove local data using the app’s settings.
